This project focuses on the development and analysis of incident response playbooks. A detailed example is provided in the "Cat & Box Scenario" playbook.

## Executive Summary

The "Cat & Box Scenario" playbook outlines incident response procedures for a specialized cardboard box manufacturer. It includes communication protocols, escalation criteria, and detailed steps for handling security incidents. The playbook ensures all stakeholders receive appropriate information based on their roles.

## Key Highlights

- **Roles and Responsibilities:**
  Clearly defined roles for the SOC team, MSSP consultant, and client stakeholders.
- **Incident Response Steps:**
  A structured approach covering detection, analysis, escalation, containment, eradication, recovery, and post-incident review.
- **Severity Levels and Escalation:**
  Criteria for determining the severity of incidents and appropriate escalation paths.
- **Communication Templates:**
  Predefined templates for notifying clients and third parties during incidents.
